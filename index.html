The Journey

7 Days
Day 1
Arrival & Grounding
Arrival and private transfer to the château. A welcome tea and seasonal brunch await you. Time to settle into your room, wander through the gardens and exhale into the rhythm of the countryside. Opening Circle for intention setting and graceful connection. An early candlelit dinner prepared with fresh regional ingredients. A quiet evening to rest and restore. A soft and elegant beginning.
Day 2
The World of Scent & Sound
Morning begins with gentle yoga and breathwork in the château garden, surrounded by birdsong and morning light. A refined drawing session exploring Still Life and Light.
Essential Oil Blending Workshop. An introduction to therapeutic grade oils and the art of intuitive formulation. You will create a personal roll on blend designed to awaken memory, emotion and inner clarity.
In the evening, a Grounding Meditation transitions into an immersive Sound Healing Ceremony held inside the château for exceptional acoustics. Singing bowls and therapeutic instruments create a deeply regulating and transformative experience guided by specialists.
Seasonal dinner served at the château. Optional French wine tasting.
Day 3
Market & Botanical Alchemy
Morning garden yoga. Excursion to the Rodez farmers market for artisanal pastries, local produce and curated herbal boutiques.
Lunch in the village. Private visit to a biodynamic medicinal herb and flower estate featuring a spiral garden.
A hands on hydrosol distillation experience introduces the refined art of botanical waters and plant extraction.
Dinner at the château, prepared with regional seasonal ingredients.
Day 4
Water, Art & Flow
Morning yoga and breathwork outdoors.
Excursion to the waterfalls of Muret le Château. A guided water meditation followed by plein air drawing and a curated picnic in nature.
Return to the château for rest and personal time. An intimate evening dinner and quiet relaxation.
Day 5
Culture & Creation
Morning yoga in the garden.
Excursion to Belcastel, one of France’s most exquisite medieval villages.
Exploration and architectural sketching followed by a refined three Michelin star course lunch celebrating French regional cuisine at its highest expression.
Return to the château for rest. Essential Oil Blending Workshop: A sophisticated Room and Fabric Spray creation session. Craft a signature scent to take home as a lasting sensory memory of your retreat.
Dinner together with soft music and relaxed elegance.
Day 6
Culture & Creation
Morning yoga in the garden.
Excursion to Belcastel, one of France’s most exquisite medieval villages.
Exploration and architectural sketching followed by a refined three Michelin star course lunch celebrating French regional cuisine at its highest expression.
Return to the château for rest. Essential Oil Blending Workshop: A sophisticated Room and Fabric Spray creation session. Craft a signature scent to take home as a lasting sensory memory of your retreat.
Dinner together with soft music and relaxed elegance.
Day 7
Integration & Farewell
Slow breakfast in the château dining room. Personal time and packing.
Closing Circle in the garden to reflect and integrate. Farewell lunch before departures
